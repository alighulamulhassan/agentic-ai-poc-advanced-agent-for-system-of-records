📅 Month 6 — Capstone: Full Agentic Application 
🎯 Goal: Apply all skills to design, build, and deploy a full agentic solution delivering tangible business value 

Either choose from real world scenarios from nhs-agentic_demo-voice-agent-mcp (1).docx 

OR chose from below 

1. RFP / Proposal Automation (Professional Services) 

Scenario: Help Accenture consultants automatically triage RFPs, summarize requirements, and suggest next steps. 
Agents: Researcher, Writer, Reviewer, Coordinator 
Features: Knowledge retrieval, multi-agent collaboration, automated document drafts 
Outcome: Reduce proposal prep time, ensure higher quality submissions 
2. Opportunity Discovery & Job Matching (Internal / HR AI) 

Scenario: Scan internal/external job boards and skills databases to match consultants to side projects. 
Agents: Data Aggregator, Matchmaker, Recommender, Alerting Agent 
Features: Embeddings for skills, vector similarity matching, notification triggers 
Outcome: Faster staffing, higher bench utilisation 
3. Client Insights & Market Research (Retail / Consumer Goods) 

Scenario: Aggregate client feedback, industry reports, and competitor activity to provide insights for decision-making. 
Agents: Web Scraper, Summarizer, Trend Analyzer, Dashboard Generator 
Features: Data ingestion, memory + retrieval, report generation 
Outcome: Actionable insights delivered automatically to client teams 
4. Policy Compliance & Risk Monitoring (Financial Services / Regulated Industries) 

Scenario: Monitor internal documents, emails, and transactions for compliance breaches or policy violations. 
Agents: Monitor Agent, Classifier Agent, Escalation Agent 
Features: Governance logs, alerting, human-in-loop approvals 
Outcome: Reduce compliance risk, speed up policy enforcement 
5. IT Ops / AIOps Automation (Technology / Cloud) 

Scenario: Automate detection and remediation of recurring cloud infrastructure or application issues. 
Agents: Event Listener, Diagnoser, Remediator, Reporting Agent 
Features: Observability, automated recovery, audit logging 
Outcome: Reduced downtime, faster incident resolution
