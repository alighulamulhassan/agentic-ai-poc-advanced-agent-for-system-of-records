# Return Policy

## Overview
We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a hassle-free return process.

## Return Window
- **Standard items**: 30 days from delivery date
- **Electronics**: 30 days from delivery date
- **Furniture**: 14 days from delivery date (must be unassembled)
- **Final sale items**: Not eligible for return

## Eligibility Requirements
To be eligible for a return, items must be:
1. In original, unused condition
2. In original packaging with all tags attached
3. Accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase
4. Free from damage, stains, or alterations

## How to Initiate a Return

### Option 1: Online Portal
1. Log into your account at our website
2. Go to "Order History"
3. Select the order containing the item(s) to return
4. Click "Start Return" and follow the prompts
5. Print the prepaid return label

### Option 2: Contact Customer Support
- **Phone**: 1-800-555-SHOP (Mon-Fri, 9am-6pm EST)
- **Email**: returns@ourstore.com
- **Chat**: Available 24/7 on our website

## Refund Process
- **Original payment method**: Refunds are issued to the original payment method
- **Processing time**: 3-5 business days after we receive the return
- **Shipping costs**: Original shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error

## Exchanges
We offer free exchanges for different sizes or colors of the same item. Simply note "Exchange" on your return form and specify the desired replacement.

## Exceptions

### Items That Cannot Be Returned
- Personalized or custom-made items
- Opened software, games, or digital content
- Intimate apparel and swimwear (for hygiene reasons)
- Hazardous materials
- Gift cards

### Damaged or Defective Items
If you receive a damaged or defective item:
1. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
2. Provide photos of the damage
3. We'll arrange a free return and replacement or full refund

## International Returns
- International customers are responsible for return shipping costs
- Customs duties and taxes are non-refundable
- Use a trackable shipping method for your protection

## Holiday Extended Returns
Orders placed between November 15 and December 31 can be returned until January 31 of the following year.
