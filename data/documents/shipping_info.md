# Shipping Information

## Shipping Options

### Standard Shipping
- **Cost**: Free on orders over $50, otherwise $5.99
- **Delivery Time**: 5-7 business days
- **Tracking**: Yes, via email and account

### Express Shipping
- **Cost**: $12.99
- **Delivery Time**: 2-3 business days
- **Tracking**: Yes, with real-time updates

### Next Day Shipping
- **Cost**: $24.99
- **Delivery Time**: Next business day (order by 2pm EST)
- **Tracking**: Yes, with real-time updates
- **Availability**: Continental US only

### Same Day Delivery
- **Cost**: $29.99
- **Delivery Time**: Same day (order by 10am local time)
- **Availability**: Select metro areas only

## Order Processing
- Orders placed before 2pm EST on business days are processed the same day
- Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed the next business day
- You'll receive an email confirmation with tracking information once shipped

## Shipping Carriers
We partner with trusted carriers including:
- FedEx
- UPS
- USPS
- DHL (for international orders)

## International Shipping
We ship to over 100 countries worldwide!

### International Rates
- Rates calculated at checkout based on destination and weight
- Typical delivery: 7-14 business days
- Express international: 3-5 business days

### Customs and Duties
- International customers are responsible for any customs duties or import taxes
- These fees are collected by your country's customs office
- We declare the full value of items on customs forms

## Tracking Your Order
1. Log into your account
2. Go to "Order History"
3. Click on your order number
4. View tracking status and carrier information

Or use the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email.

## Delivery Issues

### Package Not Received
If tracking shows delivered but you haven't received your package:
1. Check with neighbors and household members
2. Look in safe places (porch, garage, mailbox)
3. Wait 24 hours - sometimes tracking updates before delivery
4. Contact us if still not found after 24 hours

### Damaged Package
If your package arrives damaged:
1. Take photos before opening
2. Document any damage to contents
3. Contact us within 48 hours
4. We'll arrange a replacement or refund

## Address Changes
- Contact us immediately if you need to change your shipping address
- Changes can only be made before the order ships
- Once shipped, packages cannot be redirected

## P.O. Boxes and APO/FPO
- Standard shipping available to P.O. boxes
- We ship to APO/FPO addresses (delivery times may vary)
- Some oversized items cannot be shipped to P.O. boxes
